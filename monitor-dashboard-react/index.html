Risk Log

The risk log identifies potential issues that could negatively affect the successful completion of the project and outlines mitigation strategies to reduce their impact.

ID Risk Name Occurrence Impact Plan B / Mitigation Strategy
R1 Limited access to real cooperative environments Medium Medium Use simulated environments and test data; design the system so it can later be deployed in real cooperative settings.
R2 Low facial recognition accuracy in poor lighting or motion Medium High Collect training images under varied lighting conditions; tune thresholds; and clearly document limitations in the report.
R3 Hardware limitations on employee devices Medium Medium Optimise code, reduce frame rate or resolution, and ensure the system runs on standard CPUs without specialised hardware.
R4 Privacy and ethical concerns from employees Low High Process all facial data locally, avoid storing raw images, obtain informed consent, and limit system use strictly to device security.
R5 Time constraints during development phase Medium High Prioritise core features (detection, recognition, locking, logging) and treat advanced features as future work.
Table: Risk log for the facial recognition system project (cybersecurity-oriented).
